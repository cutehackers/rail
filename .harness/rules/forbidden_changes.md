# Forbidden Changes

- Do not edit generated files manually.
- Do not add new packages unless explicitly allowed.
- Do not change public interfaces unless explicitly allowed.
- Do not touch unrelated screens/features.
- Do not rewrite shared navigation shell.
- Do not modify global route/entry logic unless required by task.
- Do not perform speculative refactors.
- Do not introduce broad architecture changes during bug-fix or refactor tasks.
- Do not touch CI/build scripts/platform configs unless the task explicitly asks.
- Do not rename existing symbols broadly unless required.
- Do not add external side effects that affect unrelated areas.
